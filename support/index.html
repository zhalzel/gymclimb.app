Gym Climb Support

Help for the iPhone and Apple Watch climbing logbook.

Getting Help

For support, bug reports, or feature requests, use the public support tracker at github.com/zhalzel/gymclimb.app/issues.

Please avoid posting private health, location, account, or personal contact details in public issue reports.

What To Include

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • Whether you are also using the Apple Watch app.
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected.
  • Screenshots only if they do not reveal private details.

Privacy

Gym Climb is designed around local, private logging. Read the Gym Climb Privacy Policy.
