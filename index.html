A session should feel easy to remember.

The app is built around the rhythm of the gym: attempt, log, rest, repeat. Each entry should take seconds and still tell the story of the session later.

Start

Pick the climb

Top rope, lead, auto belay, or boulder. Grade and route color stay one tap away.

Between attempts

Log without lingering

Result, effort, and notes fit into a fast flow so your phone never becomes the workout.

Afterward

See the pattern

Today stays scannable, and older sessions stay grouped by day for a quick look back.

Your log stays yours.

No account, no ads, no cross-app tracking. Your climbing log is stored on device and can sync through your private iCloud account when available.
